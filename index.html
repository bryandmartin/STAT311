Welcome to the Lab Website for Stat 311

This website contains the labs for Stat 311 at the University of Washington. These labs were first adopted from OpenIntro for the Autumn 2019 version of Stat 311, and have been updated in Summer 2020. Comments, suggestions and contributions are welcome: send an email to bmartin6@uw.edu.

Lab Materials and Lab Schedule

  • Week 1 (06/22 – 06/26)
    • Lab 0: Downloading R and RStudio
    • Lab 1: Introduction to R, RStudio, and RMarkdown
  • Week 2 (06/29 – 07/03)
    • Lab 2: Introduction to Tidyverse
    • Lab 3: Exploring Numerical Data
